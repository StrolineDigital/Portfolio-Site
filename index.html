About Me

Hi, I'm Luke Stroehlein, a new developer with a diverse background in multiple industries. I first became interested in technology in college when I used C++ in a robotics class and also built several linux boxes. Since then I have continued to acquire skills throughout my career including updating software, performing electrical diagnosis on vehicles, and using a wide variety of point of sale and data management software.

Today I am honing my skills to excel in the world of software development. A true passion has been awakened in me, as they say “coding is art that does something” and I couldn’t agree more. I look forward to seeing what creative avenues this new path takes me toward and to grow and learn along the way.

In my free time I can be found listening to LoFi or EDM music, drinking espresso, working out, going on a road trip, or taking a walk in nature.
